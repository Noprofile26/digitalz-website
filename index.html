Therapeutic Massage

A hands-on manipulation of the soft tissues of the body, such as muscles, tendons, ligaments, and connective tissues. It is a holistic approach to health and well-being that aims to alleviate physical and emotional tension, promote relaxation, and enhance overall wellness.

Relaxation Massage

Gentle and flowing strokes are commonly used, focusing on providing a soothing experience rather than targeting specific issues or muscle tension. It's primary goal of relaxation massage is to induce a sense of calm, reduce stress, and promote overall relaxation.

Cupping

A therapeutic technique that involves placing cups on the skin to create suction. The purpose of cupping massage is to promote healing, improve blood flow, and alleviate various physical conditions. Some key purposes and benefits associated with cupping massage are Improved Blood Circulation, Muscle Tension and Pain Relief, Reduce Inflamation and many others.
